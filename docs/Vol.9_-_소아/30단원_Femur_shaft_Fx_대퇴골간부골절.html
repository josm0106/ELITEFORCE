Femur shaft fracture

대퇴골 간부 골절

교과서 내용 정리

1) Femur shaft Fracture

(1) Deforming force

• (i) prox. shaft fracture에서는 iliopsoas flexion, adduction, IR 될

• (ii) distal shaft fracture에서는 flexion, IR 될 때 shortening 및 angulation 발생

(2) Classification

• Spiral, transverse, comminuted, butterfly 등으로 분류

• Flexible intramedullary nail fixation technique (Campbell)

• F/U 시에는 cortisone inject AVN 위

2) Complications

(1) limb length discrepancy

• 10세 미만에는 깊히자 10세

• prox. 1/3 fx. 나 comminuted fx 시에 overgrowth 설린

• 대퇴 골절 후 칭 것에 증가 금제이 없니나 그 위한은 저혈 없이나

(2) angulation

• 원형 종료에 의한 대퇴의 회전 파괴가 흥 수 있다

• 대퇴 종료에 의한 대퇴의 골단 유양증

• 혈선은 2-10세, 평균 0.9cm (0.4-2.5cm) 정도 과정하

• 허용하는 종족증도가 1-1.5cm인 1 2cm까지 서울 면된

• 허용하는 2-5cm이상 히등하면 14세에서는 진저 1cm테 2cm외 허금 면된

(3) angulation

• coronal 15도(대칭 10도), sagittal 20도(대칭 30도)까지는 acceptable

• 내연 변형이 있었따면 교정이 굳찬다

(4) delayed union & nonunion

• most often after open fractures

• fractures with segmental bone loss

• soft tissue interposed between the fragments

• subtrochanteric fractures

• Tx: continuing cast immobilization until bridging callus forms

(5) 임장에 따라 허물되는 정도

나이 대축, 회판 평면 정축, 중상각 평면 단축

----------------------------------

0~2-4 AO의 AO의 15mm

2-4,4 ISC 20도 20mm

6~10세 ISC ISC 15mm

11~14~성장정지 ISC ISC 10mm

3) 치료

(1) Type I

① Open reduction 시

(2) Treatment recommendations

#### Type I

① Birth related time Fractures

② With shortening & associated complications are associated with long-term morbidity

③ Twig progression, branch presentation and presagially more associated with displacement-related bone fractures

④ In children with multiple-system trauma either flexible intramedullary nailing or external fixation is preferred.

⑤ Flexible IM nailing is the preferred method for displaced femoral shaft fractures

⑥ In children in the 5-12 year age group, flexible intramedullary nailing is preferable. This after body aligns the distal fragment with the proximal body.

#### Type II

① Adoles bone of very early. Can use either ORIF closed reduction

4) Kim's Solution

Flexible Intramedullary nail fixation technique (Campbell)

- the nails through a longitudinal drill hole, u to k mm in diameter, made in the distal femoral metaphysis just above the physic

- Leave the distal portion of the nails slightly protruding for ease of removal

- Do not bend the exposed to distal tip of the nail away from femoral metaphysis as this will irritate surrounding tissues (소자 Rockwood)

AVN risk을 감소시키기 위해 entry pointk piriformis fossa 바식 Greater trochanter tip (Campbell) or just lat (Rockwood)으로 찰다.
