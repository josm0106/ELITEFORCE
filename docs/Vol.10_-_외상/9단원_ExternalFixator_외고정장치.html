9-1. Pin site infection — 21회

교과서 내용 정리하기

1) Acceptable Indication

(1) Severe type II and III open fractures

(2) Fractures associated with severe burns

(3) Fractures requiring subsequent cross-leg flaps, free vascularized grafts, or other reconstructive procedures

(4) Certain fractures requiring distraction (e.g., fractures associated with significant bone loss or fractures in paired bones of an extremity in which maintenance of equal length of the paired bones is important)

(5) Limb lengthening

(6) Arthrodesis

(7) Infected fractures or nonunions

(8) Correction of malunions

2) Possible Indication

(1) Certain pelvic fractures and dislocations

(2) Open, infected pelvic nonunions

(3) Reconstructive pelvic osteotomy (i.e., exstrophy of the bladder)

(4) Fixation after radical tumor excision with autograft or allograft replacement

(5) Femoral osteotomies in children (use of this method eliminates the necessity of subsequent removal of internal fixation appliances such as plates and screws)

(6) Fractures associated with vascular or nerve repairs or reconstructions

(7) Limb reimplantation

3) Design and Application of EF

(1) Type of EF

① pin fixators

- simple fixator: provide independent application of single pins

- clamp fixator: permit spatially constrained pin clusters

② ring fixators

- complete or partial rings connected by rods or articulated member

(2) Pin Fixator의 4 Basic Configurations

① Unilateral uniplanar (가장 흔히 사용)

② Unilateral biplanar

③ Bilateral uniplanar (V-frame)

④ Bilateral biplanar

(3) Stiffness에 영향을 주는 인자

① Pin diameter가 커질수록 stiffness 증가

② Pin을 bone에 가까이 할수록 stiffness 증가

③ Pin 수가 많을수록 stiffness 증가

④ Pin spacing이 넓을수록 stiffness 증가

⑤ Bone-rod distance가 가까울수록 stiffness 증가

(4~5) Combinations / Tensioned Wire Fixators

(6) Combinations of internal and external fixation

① effective for stabilization of severely comminuted fractures

② advantage:

I) anatomical stable fixation

II) less soft tissue dissection

III) no large implants

③ not used this technique in diaphyseal fractures, which usually can be adequately stabilized with standard internal or external fixation methods

(7) Tensioned wire fixators

- Angulatory, translation, rotational, and length deformities가 교정 가능

(8) General Method for Half-Pin Fixator

① skin sharply incised and gentle blunt dissection to bone in safe zone

② hand drilling 이나 low-speed power drilling을 사용하여 thermal necrosis를 줄여라

③ Predrilling reduces bone temperature by approximately 50%

④ Half-pins have a wide range of diameter ranging from 2mm to 6mm with all intermediate sizes available (6mm이상 넓으면 bone을 약화시키는 결과 초래)

(9) General Method for Circular Wire Fixators

① In general, 1.5mm and 1.8mm wires need no incision or drill sheath

② 일반적으로 2mm보다 큰 wire 사용할 때만 sheath 와 skin incision을 사용

③ Olive wires require a small incision through the skin only

④ Wires are used that have a special self-drilling tip so that no predrilling is required

⑤ In general, large fracture fragments require two levels of fixation with two wires at each level

⑥ Short fragments can be fixed with one ring and a drop wire or with a wire offset from the main ring by a few centimeters

⑦ Stability is increased by increasing the angle

3) Complications

(1) Pin Track Infection: m/c (30%)

★ Pin track infection이 m/c complication이며 약 30%에서 발생

① minor inflammation: local wound care

② superficial infection: antibiotics, local wound care, occasional pin removal

③ osteomyelitis: sequestrectomy

(2) Neurovascular Impairment

① forearm prox. 1/2 & arm distal 1/2: radial nerve

② wrist joint 내측 삽입: dorsal sensory radial nerve

③ leg distal 1/3 & 1/4 junction: anterior tibial a. & deep peroneal n.

(3) Muscle or Tendon Entrapment

- Ankle stiffness is frequent if multiple transfixing pins are used in fractures of the tibia

- Limbs must be placed in a position that avoids contractures before wires or pins are inserted impaling tendons and muscle

(4) Delayed Union

- The rigid pins and frames can 'unload' the fracture site

- Delayed unions in 20% to 30% (up to 80%) of fractures have been reported in the literature with prolonged use of the rigid fixator

(5) Compartment Syndrome

(6) Refracture

- Union resulting from the rigid fixation is largely endosteal, with little peripheral callus formation

- The destressing of the cortical bone by the rigid fixation results in cancellization of the cortex

- Refracture is possible after fixator removal

(7) Limitation of Future Alternatives

- Such methods as open reduction become difficult or impossible if pin tracks become infected

★ 외고정후 내고정으로 전환하는 적절한 시기에 대해서는 2주 이내에, 금속핀이나 비확공성 골수강 내 금속정 삽입은 비교적 안전하다.

📌 Take-Home Message

1. EF acceptable indication: severe open fx (type II,III), severe burns, bone loss requiring distraction, limb lengthening, arthrodesis, infected nonunion, malunion correction.

2. Pin fixator 4 configurations: unilateral uniplanar(m/c), unilateral biplanar, bilateral uniplanar(V-frame), bilateral biplanar.

3. Pin track infection: m/c complication (30%). Minor→local wound care, Superficial→antibiotics, Osteomyelitis→sequestrectomy.

4. Neurovascular impairment: forearm prox 1/2→radial n., leg distal 1/3-1/4 junction→anterior tibial a. & deep peroneal n.

5. Delayed union: 20-30%(최대 80%), rigid fixator의 prolonged use시.

6. EF→내고정 전환: 2주 이내에 비확공성 IM nail 삽입이 비교적 안전.

7. Predrilling으로 bone temperature를 약 50% 감소시킬 수 있다.

✏️ 단원 연습 문제

Q1. Pin Track Infection

외고정 장치의 가장 흔한 합병증은?

① Delayed union

② Pin track infection

③ Compartment syndrome

④ Refracture

⑤ Neurovascular impairment

▶ 정답: ②

해설: Pin track infection이 m/c complication으로 약 30%에서 발생한다.

Q2. EF의 Acceptable Indication

다음 중 external fixator의 acceptable indication이 아닌 것은?

① Severe type III open fracture

② Fractures with severe burns

③ Simple transverse femoral shaft fracture

④ Infected nonunion

⑤ Limb lengthening

▶ 정답: ③

해설: Simple transverse femoral shaft fracture는 IM nail 등으로 치료하며 EF의 일반적 적응증이 아니다. Diaphyseal fracture에는 standard internal or external fixation으로 충분하다.

Q3. Neurovascular Impairment

경골 하 1/3과 1/4 junction에서 external fixator pin 삽입 시 손상되기 쉬운 구조물은?

① Radial nerve

② Dorsal sensory radial nerve

③ Anterior tibial a. & deep peroneal n.

④ Superficial peroneal nerve

⑤ Posterior tibial a.

▶ 정답: ③

해설: Leg distal 1/3 & 1/4 junction에서는 anterior tibial artery와 deep peroneal nerve가 손상되기 쉽다.

Q4. Predrilling 효과

Half-pin fixator 삽입 시 predrilling의 효과는?

① Bone temperature를 약 25% 감소

② Bone temperature를 약 50% 감소

③ Bone temperature를 약 75% 감소

④ Pin pull-out strength 증가

⑤ Pin track infection 완전 예방

▶ 정답: ②

해설: Predrilling은 bone temperature를 약 50% 감소시킨다.

Q5. EF에서 내고정 전환

외고정 장치 후 내고정으로 전환하는 적절한 시기는?

① 1주 이내

② 2주 이내

③ 4주 이내

④ 6주 이내

⑤ 시기에 관계없다

▶ 정답: ②

해설: 외고정후 내고정으로 전환하는 적절한 시기는 2주 이내에, 금속핀이나 비확공성 골수강 내 금속정 삽입이 비교적 안전하다.
