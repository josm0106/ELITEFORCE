2) Operative indication

(1) Displacement > 2mm (2) Roof arc angle < 45° (3) CT 초기 50% 이상 벽이 이상되는 stress test(응 않는다) (4) post. wall Fx+1 50% 이상의 정합도 stress(축함) (5) post. wall Fx+1 50% 미만의 정합도 stress test(축함) (6) 나쁜 자세등 수술 전 정합 (7) 오동성 손상등 위험한 정합 (8) 나쁜 정합등 술전 정합보

3) Timing of surgery

- OR/IP : should be performed within 5 to 7 days of injury - more difficult after that time because hematoma organization, soft tissue contracture, and subsequent early callus formation hinder the process of fracture reduction, especially if more limited exposures are used.

(4) Choice of surgical approach

(1) Kocher-Langenbeck approach - post. wall & post. column Fx, transverse Fx, post. wall Fx + T-shape Fx - Kocher-Langenbeck approach provides direct visualization of the entire lateral aspect of the posterior column of the acetabulum. (2) Ilioinguinal approach - ant. iliac form & iliac crest, ant. wall, transverse Fx., both column Fx의 기능 - Ilioinguinal approach allows access to the internal aspect of the innominate bone from the ischium area to the ilium pubis. - Transverse incisions, inner oblique, ant. wall & post. wall fractures - This approach is felt to be advantageous because it offers improved exposure of the quadrilateral surface and posterior column while minimizing dissection in the area of the "middle window" of the ilioinguinal approach. - It is especially useful for fractures requiring buttress plating of the quadrilateral - The advantage over the ilioinguinal is that direction of the vessels is not required. - However, this lack of access to the ilioinguinal "middle window" is also its disadvantage. - With this approach, it is particularly important to locate the obturator nerve - obturator neurovascular bundle crosses the quadrilateral surface and, in some cases, it should be mobilized.

6) 비구골절 특수 병판 위치별 접근:

① Anterior iliac wing fracture (iliac wing) - fracture of one or both columns with extension into the ilium due to indirect trauma from reduction secondary to lateral force transmission. - Direct visualization of the fracture is achieved by the ilioinguinal approach. - This approach does not allow direct access to the hip joint. - Fractures requiring direct visualization for removal of intra-articular loose bodies or for fractures with secondary dorsal muscle mass extending muscle. - The greater sciatica notches are visualized by transecting the gluteus and obturator internus tendons and by releasing the abductor. - Visualization may be extended interoperatively by dividing a portion of the gluteus medius - improves exposure - Transection of the retinacular vessels does not prevent the femoral head from fully seating into the acetabulum - The buttress plate is best placed parallel and close to the rim of the acetabulum. - Where it can provide the best buttress for the wall fragments - Mostly, three screws are placed cephalad to the fracture fragments and two placed distal. - with one of them being a long screw into the ischium. - 정합 정합되기 중국 한 정합 중요하고 고정된 것 중계동점 피하기 정합이

7) TABLE 4-7 ORF of Posterior Wall Fractures Surgical Steps

- Expose and identify fracture fragments - Protect sciatic nerve - Carefully disengage posterior wall segments while maintaining capsular attachments - Identify and remove intra-articular debris - Reduce femoral head into acetabulum - Manually reposition femoral head into fragments - Identify, label of marginal impaction - Using the femoral head as a template - Elevate and reduce marginal impaction - Clean and remove osteochondral fragments - Fill underlying bone void with graft material - Replace fractures with lacrimal/cortical pegs or prostheses, as needed - Individually fix the nail fragments using lag screws without excessive tightening - Buttress the important using a well-contoured 3.5-mm reconstruction plate - Fully tighten all lag screws

8) 비구골절 합병증

① 감염 - 빈도: 4.2-5% (깊은 감염은 0.5-3%) - 원인: 개방골절의 더 높은 감염률, 광범위 연부조직 손상 등 ② 신경 손상 - 좌골신경 손상 (10% 정도) - 골반신경 손상: 후벽 동반 50-90% - 대퇴 외측 피부신경 손상(tibia fibronectin personol division) 정법 - Lateral femoral cutaneous nerve는 보존적 치료 및 보존기 관절의 격렬히 없거나 약간 생김 ③ Heterotopic ossification - Kocher-Langenbeck approach ≥25% (5-9번안) - 보 다음의 방수류 이미 약정될 수 있는 미중 - indomethacin, 750mJy BTx - 고해점 기능이 심하면 영향을 상하는 거부면 정합 손상이 초래 중류

1. Modified stoppa approach에 quadrilateral surface 접근이 가장 잘 이루어질 수 있는 이유는?

① Posterior column Fx. + post. wall Fx.에 적합 ② Transverse Fx.에 적합 ③ 두 개 모두 가능 ④ 수술 정합 선택 기준 ⑤ Ilioingunial approach 우월
