Tuberculosis spinal infection

결핵성 척추 감염

Ch32. 결핵성 척추 감염 (Tuberculosis Spinal Infection)

1) 척추 결핵의 개요

#### (1) 정의 및 역학

**I. 기본 개념**

척추 결핵(Spinal Tuberculosis)은 Mycobacterium tuberculosis에 의한 척추의 만성 감염으로, 다음과 같은 특징을 가집니다:

1. **역학적 특징**

• 결핵성 골질환 중 가장 흔한 부위

• 척추 감염의 대표적 질환

• Global health problem

• 특히 개발도상국에서 높은 유병률

2. **감염 경로**

• Hematogenous spread (주요 경로)

• 폐결핵 또는 폐외결핵으로부터 전파

• 척추체로의 직접 침범

3. **주요 특징**

• Slow, progressive disease

• Multiple segment involvement

• Paravertebral abscess formation

• Spinal deformity (kyphosis)

• Neurological complications 가능

#### (2) 결핵성 척추 감염의 분류

**I. 병변 부위에 따른 분류**

1. **Anterior vertebral body involvement (척추체 앞부분 침범)**

• 가장 흔한 형태 (90% 이상)

• Lower thoracic 또는 upper lumbar segments

• Progressive destruction

2. **Central body involvement (척추체 중앙 침범)**

• Vertebral hemangioma 같은 appearance

• Less common

3. **Posterior element involvement (척추 후방 구조 침범)**

• 드문 형태

• Lamina, spinous process, facet joint involvement

• Neural arch destruction

#### (3) 원인 및 위험 인자

**I. 감염원**

1. **Primary focus**

• Pulmonary TB (폐결핵): 가장 흔한 source

• Extrapulmonary TB (폐외결핵)

• Lymph node TB

• Miliary TB

2. **전파 경로**

• Hematogenous dissemination

• Vertebral blood supply를 통한 직접 침범

• Lymphatic spread (드문)

• Contiguous spread from thoracic disease

**II. 위험 인자**

1. **면역 관련**

• HIV/AIDS

• Immunosuppressive therapy

• Malnutrition

2. **사회경제적 요인**

• Poor housing conditions

• Overcrowding

• Low socioeconomic status

• Poor nutritional status

3. **의료 관련**

• Delayed diagnosis

• Inadequate treatment

• Drug resistance

2) 병리 및 자연사

#### (1) 병리학적 변화

**I. 초기 병변**

1. **혈행성 침범**

• Vertebral body의 metaphyseal region에서 시작

• Anterior vertebral body 침범 (most common)

• Lower thoracic 또는 upper lumbar segment 호발

2. **초기 감염 부위의 특징**

• Paradiscal location: 척추체-추간판 경계

• Inflammatory infiltration

• Granulation tissue formation

• Early necrosis

#### (2) 질환의 진행

**I. 진행 단계**

1. **Stage 1: Early disease (초기)**

• Vertebral body inflammation

• Minimal destruction

• No significant deformity

• Disc space may be intact initially

2. **Stage 2: Active disease (활동기)**

• Progressive vertebral body destruction

• Anterior vertebral collapse

• Paravertebral abscess formation

• Disc space involvement (late)

• Angular kyphosis development

3. **Stage 3: Healed stage (치유기)**

• Fibrosis and sclerosis

• Vertebral fusion

• Residual deformity

• Scar formation

#### (3) 합병증의 발생

**I. Kyphotic deformity 진행**

1. **조기 후만증 (Early kyphosis)**

• During active disease

• Progressive vertebral destruction

• Wedge-shaped vertebrae

• Multiple level involvement

2. **만기 후만증 (Late kyphosis)**

• During healing phase

• Fibrosis and contracture

• Vertebral fusion in deformed position

• Can progress even after healing

3. **척추변형의 결과**

• Cosmetic deformity

• Functional impairment

• Restrictive lung disease (thoracic TB)

• Neurological compromise

**II. Pott's paraplegia (포트 마비)**

**I. 정의 및 분류**

Pott's paraplegia는 척추 결핵으로 인한 척수 마비 또는 신경근 증상으로, 다음과 같이 분류됩니다:

1. **조기 마비 (Early-onset paraplegia, active disease)**

• Active TB disease 동안 발생

• 진행 중인 척수 압박

• 척추 불안정성

• Active disease와 동시에 발생

2. **만기 마비 (Late-onset paraplegia, healed disease)**

• TB가 치유된 후 발생

• Post-healing complications

• Mechanical compression

• Spinal cord fibrosis

• Vertebral ankylosis로 인한 compression

#### (4) 신경학적 손상의 기전

**I. 조기 마비의 원인**

1. **Active disease 관련 기전:**

• Inflammatory edema (염증성 부종)

• Spinal cord inflammation (척수염)

• Granulation tissue invasion

• Active abscess

• Instability at disease site

2. **기구적 압박:**

• Paravertebral abscess

• Caseous material

• Inflammatory granulation tissue

• Vertebral subluxation

**II. 만기 마비의 원인**

1. **기구적 원인 (Mechanical causes)**

• Vertebral ankylosis (척추 유착)

• Fixed kyphosis (고정된 후만증)

• Fibrosis and scar tissue

• Disc herniation in healed disease

• Bony ridge formation

2. **혈관학적 원인 (Vascular causes)**

• Spinal artery occlusion

• Ischemic myelopathy

• Penetration of fibrosis through dura

• Transection of cord by bony ridge

3. **추가 원인:**

• Spinal cord atrophy

• Cord tethering

• Arachnoiditis

#### (5) 신경학적 손상의 위험 요인

**I. 조기 마비의 위험 요인**

• Age 24세 이상

• Presence of comorbidities

• Active disease at higher thoracic levels

• Spinal instability

• Mechanical factors

**II. 만기 마비의 위험 요인**

• Severe kyphotic deformity

• Long disease duration

• Multiple vertebral involvement

• Spinal cord atrophy

• Presence of epidural fibrosis

3) 임상 특징

#### (1) 임상 증상

**I. 초기 증상**

1. **척추 증상**

• Insidious onset pain (서서히 발생하는 통증)

• Constitutional symptoms

• Fever (low-grade, evening)

• Night sweats

• Weight loss

• Fatigue

2. **국소 증상**

• Back pain at disease site

• Gibbus formation (척추 고정)

• Progressive kyphosis

• Spinal stiffness

3. **신경학적 증상**

• May be absent initially

• Radiculopathy (신경근병증)

• Myelopathy (척수병증)

• Weakness

• Sensory changes

#### (2) 진단 기준

**I. Modified New York Criteria-like evaluation**

1. **병력 평가**

• TB 접촉 여부

• Constitutional symptoms

• Duration of symptoms

• Prior TB treatment

2. **신체 검진**

• Local tenderness at disease site

• Gibbus deformity

• Neurological deficit assessment

• Fever presence

3. **실험실 검사**

• Sputum AFB (폐결핵 평가)

• Tuberculin skin test (Mantoux)

• ESR elevation

• CRP elevation

4. **방사선 및 영상**

• Plain radiographs

• CT scan

• MRI (best modality)

4) 방사선학적 소견

#### (1) Plain radiographs

**I. 척추 X-ray 특징적 소견**

1. **초기 소견**

• Anterior vertebral body involvement

• Rarefaction of vertebral body

• Subtle end-plate changes

• Early wedge deformity

2. **진행된 소견**

• Progressive vertebral body destruction

• Anterior vertebral collapse

• Wedge-shaped deformity

• Angular kyphosis

• "Skip lesions" (병변 건너뛰기)

• Paravertebral soft tissue shadow

3. **후기 소견**

• Vertebral fusion

• Severe kyphosis

• Sclerotic margins

• Healed appearance

• Residual deformity

#### (2) CT Imaging

1. **장점**

• Better bone detail

• Abscess localization

• Soft tissue assessment

• Canal compromise evaluation

• Instability assessment

2. **특징적 소견**

• Anterior vertebral involvement

• Paraspinal abscess

• Locked vertebrae appearance

• Posterior longitudinal ligament ossification

• Spinal canal compromise

#### (3) MRI Findings

**I. T1-weighted images**

1. **특징**

• Hypointense vertebral bodies

• Loss of normal marrow signal

• Abscess: low signal initially

• T1 post-gadolinium: enhancement of granulation tissue

2. **병변 특징**

• Anterior vertebral body involvement (characteristic)

• Subligamentous spread

• Preservation of disc space (initially)

• Multisegmental involvement

**II. T2-weighted images**

1. **특징**

• Hyperintense areas (edema, inflammatory tissue)

• Abscess: high signal (caseous material)

• Spinal cord signal changes

• Myelitis: hyperintense cord signal

**III. STIR 시퀀스**

• Hypersensitivity to water (부종과 염증에 민감)

• Abscess: high signal

• Inflammatory tissue: high signal

• Sensitive for early detection

**IV. Post-gadolinium images**

• Enhancement of granulation tissue

• Differentiation of active disease from fibrosis

• Assessment of spinal cord involvement

• Epidural inflammation visualization

#### (4) MRI의 우월성

MRI는 결핵성 척추 감염의 진단에서:

• Soft tissue 평가 우수

• Spinal cord status 정확한 평가

• Subligamentous spread 확인

• Paravertebral abscess 정확한 위치 파악

• Early changes 감지

• Neurological complication assessment

5) 치료 방침

#### (1) 의학적 치료 (Medical Management)

**I. 항결핵 약물 (Anti-tuberculous drugs)**

1. **1차 약제 (First-line agents)**

• INH (Isoniazid)

• RFP (Rifampicin)

• EMB (Ethambutol)

• PZA (Pyrazinamide)

• Standard therapy: INH, RFP, EMB, PZA로 시작

2. **2차 약제 (Second-line agents)**

• Fluoroquinolones

• Ethionamide

• Prothionamide

• Cycloserine

• Para-aminosalicylic acid (PAS)

3. **치료 기간**

• Intensive phase: 2개월 (4제 투여)

• Continuation phase: 4개월 (INH, RFP)

• 총 6개월 (표준 치료)

• Drug-resistant TB: 더 오래 필요 (18-24개월)

#### (2) 보조 치료

**I. 비수술적 치료 (Conservative Management)**

1. **초기 관리**

• Anti-TB drugs 시작

• Nutritional support

• Rest and immobilization

• Pain control

• Monitoring for complications

2. **추적 관찰**

• Clinical assessment

• Imaging surveillance

• Neurological monitoring

• Treatment response evaluation

3. **생활 습관 관리**

• 영양 섭취 개선

• 휴식 및 활동 조절

• 정기적 X-ray 추적

#### (3) 수술적 치료 (Operative Treatment)

**I. 수술 적응증**

1. **신경학적 합병증**

• Active disease with myelopathy

• Progressive neurological deficit

• Pott's paraplegia (조기 또는 만기)

• Cauda equina involvement

2. **구조적 불안정성**

• Severe kyphosis threatening spinal cord

• Spinal instability

• Fracture-dislocation

• Progressive deformity

3. **치료 실패**

• Medical therapy failure

• Abscess not responding to drugs

• Persistent infection

• Severe pain uncontrolled by medical therapy

**II. 수술 기법**

1. **Anterior approach (ventralis)**

• Anterior vertebral body debridement

• Abscess drainage

• Kyphosis correction

• Anterior fusion

• Most common approach

2. **Posterior approach**

• Posterior decompression

• Laminectomy

• Posterior fusion

• Used when anterior disease not accessible

3. **Combined approach (Anterior + Posterior)**

• Severe multilevel disease

• Significant kyphosis

• Neurological compromise

• Severe instability

• Staged procedures

**III. 구체적 절차**

1. **Anterior fusion**

• Anterior vertebrectomy

• Disease debridement

• Graft placement (autograft or allograft)

• Instrumentation if needed

2. **Posterior instrumentation**

• Pedicle screw fixation

• Posterior longitudinal ligament resection if needed

• Restoration of spinal alignment

• Rigid fixation for unstable cases

6) 특수 상황

#### (1) Pott's paraplegia의 관리

**I. 조기 마비 (Early-onset)**

1. **병인**

• Active disease related

• Inflammatory compression

• Abscess

• Instability

2. **관리**

• Anti-TB therapy 우선

• Neurological monitoring

• Surgery if medical therapy fails

• Decompression if mechanical block

**II. 만기 마비 (Late-onset)**

1. **병인**

• Post-healing complications

• Fixed kyphosis

• Fibrosis and scar

• Spinal cord damage

2. **관리**

• Surgical intervention usually needed

• Kyphosis correction

• Spinal cord decompression

• Combined anterior + posterior approach

#### (2) Abscess drainage 위치별 관리

**I. Abscess 배액 부위에 따른 분류**

1. **경추 (Cervical)**

• Posterior triangle of neck

• Retropharyngeal region

• Supraclavicular region

• Mediastinum

2. **흉추 (Thoracic)**

• Pleural cavity → rib 분절

• Empyema or rib tuberculosis

• Mediastinal abscess

3. **요추 (Lumbar spine)**

• Inguinal

• Gluteal

• Psoas

• Petit triangle의 경계

• Lateral margin of LD

• Medial border of obliquus externus abdominis muscle

• Base by iliac crest

• Floor: Obliquus internus abdominis M.

7) 예후 및 장기 결과

#### (1) 치료 성공률**

• Anti-TB therapy response: 대부분의 환자에서 우수

• Neurological recovery: 신속한 항TB치료로 호전

• Healed lesions: 안정적인 상태 유지

#### (2) 합병증 및 예후 영향 인자**

1. **양호한 예후 인자**

• Early diagnosis

• Adequate anti-TB therapy

• Good nutritional status

• Absence of severe comorbidity

2. **불량한 예후 인자**

• Late diagnosis

• Severe kyphosis

• Pott's paraplegia

• Drug resistance

• Poor compliance

• Comorbidity (HIV/AIDS)

Kim's Solution

척추 결핵의 진단 및 치료 전략

**척추 결핵 환자의 주요 특징:**

1) 65세 여자, 2개월 진행성 하반신 마비

2) T11-L2 70도 kyphosis와 T12 vertebral body collapse 동반

3) 진행하는 마비: Pott's paraplegia 시사

- 기저 결핵성 척추염과 심한 후만증으로 인한 신경 압박

- 진행성 마비는 수술 적응증

- Anterior + posterior fusion 필요
