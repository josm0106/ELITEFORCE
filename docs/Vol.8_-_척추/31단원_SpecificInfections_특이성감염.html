Ch31. 척추의 특이성 감염 (Specific Infections)

1) 척추 감염의 특이적 형태

#### (1) Pyogenic 척추 감염

**I. 정의 및 특징**

Pyogenic infections (화농성 감염)은 일반적인 세균에 의한 척추 감염으로 다음과 같은 특징을 가집니다:

1. **병원체**

• S. aureus (most common)

• Streptococcus pneumoniae

• Gram-negative organisms

• Mixed flora in some cases

2. **발생 기전**

• Hematogenous spread (혈행성 전파)

• Direct inoculation (직접 접종)

• Contiguous spread (인접 구조에서 파급)

3. **위험 인자**

• IV drug abuse

• Immunocompromised state

• Diabetes mellitus

• Chronic infections

4. **임상 특징**

• Acute presentation

• Rapid progression

• High fever

• Severe pain

• Possible neurological deficit

#### (2) Disc space infection in adults

**I. 특징**

1. **정의**

• Adult vertebral osteomyelitis와 관련된 감염

• 추간판 공간에 국소화된 감염

2. **발생 메커니즘**

• Hematogenous spread는 드물 것

• Direct inoculation이 주 경로 (수술 후, 주사)

• Procedural risk와 관련

3. **병인**

• Surgical trauma

• Needle penetration

• Post-operative contamination

4. **임상 경과**

• Delayed presentation

• Subacute symptoms

• Chronic pain

• Progressive spinal dysfunction

5. **방사선 소견**

• Disc space narrowing

• Endplate erosion

• Vertebral body involvement

• Late deformity (kyphosis)

#### (3) Epidural space infection

**I. 특징**

1. **정의 및 발생**

• Epidural abscess formation

• Spinal cord compression risk

• Emergency condition

2. **원인**

• Extension from vertebral infection

• Direct hematogenous seeding

• Post-operative contamination

• Post-traumatic infection

3. **임상 증상**

• Acute back pain

• Neurological deficit

• Lower extremity weakness

• Sensory loss

• Bowel/bladder dysfunction

4. **진단**

• MRI: gold standard

• T1-weighted: low signal

• T2-weighted: variable signal

• Gadolinium enhancement

• Spinal cord compression evident

5. **치료**

• Emergency decompression

• Surgical drainage

• Antibiotic therapy

• Neurological monitoring

2) 특수 감염 형태

#### (1) Fungal infections (진균성 감염)

**I. 특징**

1. **병원체**

• Coccidioides immitis

• Blastomyces dermatitidis

• Cryptococcus neoformans

• Aspergillus species

• Candida species

2. **위험 인자**

• Immunocompromised state (HIV/AIDS)

• Chronic corticosteroid use

• Organ transplantation

• Malignancy

3. **임상 특징**

• Indolent course

• Gradual symptom progression

• Constitutional symptoms

• May mimic tuberculosis

4. **방사선 소견**

• Similar to tuberculosis

• Anterior vertebral body involvement

• Paravertebral abscess

• Kyphosis formation

• May show more rapid destruction

5. **진단**

• Culture from abscess aspirate

• Histopathology (granulomas)

• Antigen detection

• Serology (fungal markers)

6. **치료**

• Antifungal medications (Amphotericin B, Fluconazole)

• Surgical drainage

• Long-term suppressive therapy

• May require fusion for stability

#### (2) Childhood discitis syndrome (소아 척추염)

**I. 정의 및 특징**

1. **기본 개념**

• Pediatric-specific infection

• Affect primarily disc space

• Self-limited course in many cases

• May have neurological complications

2. **임상 특징**

• Young children primarily

• Acute onset

• Severe pain (often unable to walk)

• May appear systemically well

• Fever may be low-grade or absent

3. **방사선 소견**

• Disc space narrowing

• Endplate irregularities

• May show early vertebral involvement

• Progression to vertebral osteomyelitis possible

4. **미생물학**

• S. aureus (most common)

• Hemophilus influenzae (vaccination changed frequency)

• Group B Streptococcus

• Gram-negative organisms

5. **치료**

• Often self-limited

• Antibiotic therapy

• NSAIDs for pain control

• Spinal immobilization if unstable

• Rarely requires surgery

#### (3) Non-tuberculous mycobacterial infections

**I. 특징**

1. **병원체**

• Mycobacterium avium complex (MAC)

• Mycobacterium marinum

• Mycobacterium chelonae

• Other atypical mycobacteria

2. **위험 인자**

• HIV/AIDS

• Immunosuppressive therapy

• Organ transplantation

3. **임상 특징**

• Indolent course

• Progressive symptoms

• May present as spinal tuberculosis mimic

4. **방사선 소견**

• Similar to TB

• Anterior vertebral body destruction

• Paravertebral abscess

• Spinal deformity

5. **치료**

• Multi-drug regimens

• Clarithromycin (standard)

• Fluoroquinolones

• Isoniazid

• Extended therapy (months to years)

• Surgical drainage if indicated

#### (4) Vertebral osteomyelitis in IV drug abusers

**I. 특징**

1. **역학**

• Specific subset of injection drug users

• High prevalence in this population

• Often delayed diagnosis

2. **미생물학**

• Pseudomonas aeruginosa (common)

• S. aureus

• Fungal organisms (less common)

• Polymicrobial infections

3. **특이한 부위**

• Lumbar spine (common)

• Thoracic spine

• Thoracolumbar junction

• Unusual levels compared to non-users

4. **임상 경과**

• Often indolent

• May present with paraspinal abscess

• High rate of complications

• Poor compliance with treatment

5. **치료**

• Long-term antibiotics (6+ weeks)

• Surgical intervention often needed

• Paraspinal abscess drainage

• Spinal fusion if unstable

3) 진단 및 감별

#### (1) Diagnostic approach

**I. 임상 평가**

1. **병력**

• Risk factor assessment

• Infection source identification

• Timeline of symptom development

• Constitutional symptoms

2. **신체 검진**

• Local tenderness

• Fever presence

• Neurological assessment

• Systemic signs

#### (2) Laboratory studies

1. **Serological tests**

• Blood cultures (multiple sets)

• ESR elevation

• CRP elevation

• WBC count

• Specific antigen/antibody tests (fungal)

2. **Tissue diagnosis**

• Needle biopsy

• Surgical biopsy

• Culture and sensitivity

• Histopathology

• PCR for organisms

#### (3) Imaging

1. **Plain radiographs**

• Initial screening

• Baseline assessment

• Serial monitoring

2. **CT scanning**

• Better bone detail

• Abscess localization

• Soft tissue assessment

3. **MRI**

• Best soft tissue detail

• Spinal cord assessment

• Early changes detection

• Multi-planar imaging

4) 치료 원칙

#### (1) 비수술적 치료

1. **항생제/항진균제 치료**

• Organism-specific agents

• Long-term therapy (weeks to months)

• Serial imaging for response assessment

• Compliance monitoring

2. **보조 치료**

• NSAIDs for pain control

• Spinal immobilization

• Physical rehabilitation

• Nutritional support

#### (2) 수술적 치료

**적응증:**

• Epidural abscess with neurological deficit

• Failed medical management

• Spinal instability

• Large paravertebral abscess

• Severe deformity

**절차:**

• Drainage of abscess

• Debridement of infected tissue

• Spinal decompression

• Stabilization if needed

• Combined anterior/posterior approach if necessary

척추 특이성 감염의 진단 및 관리

**특이성 감염의 특징적 소견:**

- Fungal infections: indolent course, immunocompromised patients, anterior involvement

- Mycobacterial infections: TB-like appearance, slow progression, paravertebral abscess

- Childhood discitis: disc space narrowing, self-limited, may resolve spontaneously

- IV drug abuse related: unusual sites, aggressive organisms (Pseudomonas), poor treatment response

연습문제

1. 척추의 특이성 감염 중 가장 빠른 진행을 보이는 것은? 13B/13B

① Fungal infection

② Tuberculous infection

③ Pyogenic infection

④ Mycobacterial infection

답: ③

<Kim's solution>

척추 감염의 진행 속도:

• Pyogenic (화농성): 급성 진행

• Tuberculosis: 만성 느린 진행

• Fungal: 비정상적 진행 (indolent)

• 일반적으로 pyogenic이 가장 빠른 진행

2. 소아 척추염(Childhood discitis)의 가장 흔한 특징은? 13B/13B

① 척추체 파괴

② 추간판 협착 (disc space narrowing)

③ 경막외 농양

④ 척추 후만증

답: ②

<Kim's solution>

소아 척추염의 특징:

• 주로 추간판 공간에 국소화

• Disc space narrowing이 특징적

• 많은 경우 자연 치유됨

• 신경학적 합병증 가능

3. IV drug abuse와 관련된 척추 감염의 특징 원인 병원체는? 13B/13B

① S. aureus

② Pseudomonas aeruginosa

③ Mycobacterium tuberculosis

④ Fungal organisms

답: ②

<Kim's solution>

IV drug abuser의 척추 감염:

• Pseudomonas aeruginosa: 가장 흔함

• S. aureus: 일반 인구에서 m/c

• Fungal: 면역저하 환자

• 비정상적 척추 부위에 발생

4. Fungal spine infection의 위험 인자로 포함되지 않는 것은? 13B/13B

① HIV/AIDS

② 만성 스테로이드 사용

③ 장기 이식

④ 운동 부족

답: ④

<Kim's solution>

Fungal spine infection의 위험 인자:

• 면역저하: HIV, AIDS

• 약물: 만성 스테로이드

• 의료 시술: 장기 이식

• 운동 부족은 위험 인자 아님

5. 척추 감염에서 DM (Epidural abscess)의 가장 흔한 합병증은? 13B/13B

① Disc herniation

② Spinal cord compression

③ Vertebral collapse

④ Deformity

답: ②

<Kim's solution>

Epidural abscess의 주요 합병증:

• 경막외 농양이 척수 압박

• 신경학적 손상 위험 높음

• 응급 상황 (emergency condition)

• 신속한 수술적 감압 필요

5) 임상 관리의 실제

#### (1) 진단 알고리즘

**I. 척추 감염 의심 시 진단 접근**

1. **초기 평가 (First-line assessment)**

• Complete history and physical examination

• Blood cultures (before antibiotics)

• ESR, CRP measurement

• Complete blood count

• Plain radiographs

2. **확진 검사 (Confirmatory tests)**

• MRI: primary imaging modality

• CT: bone detail 필요 시

• Biopsy: culture와 histology 위해

3. **균동정 (Organism identification)**

• Blood culture (경험적)

• Tissue culture (gold standard)

• PCR and molecular methods

• Culture sensitivity

#### (2) 치료 의사 결정

**I. 보존적 vs. 수술적 치료의 선택**

1. **보존적 치료의 조건**

• Early diagnosis (초기 진단)

• Intact neurological status

• No significant instability

• Good compliance expected

• Organism susceptibility to drugs

2. **수술적 치료의 조건**

• Neurological deficit

• Spinal instability

• Large abscess

• Failed medical therapy

• Significant deformity

#### (3) 약물 선택의 원칙

**I. 초기 경험적 항생제 (Empiric antibiotics)**

1. **S. aureus 커버 필수**

• Cefazolin or Nafcillin

• Vancomycin (MRSA 고려 시)

• Fluoroquinolone (일부)

2. **Gram-negative coverage**

• Ceftriaxone or Cefotaxime

• Fluoroquinolone

• 필요시 aminoglycoside

3. **치료 기간**

• Minimum 6-8 weeks

• 일부는 10-12주

• Organism과 response에 따라 결정

6) 특수 치료 상황

#### (1) 외과적 감염 (Iatrogenic infections)**

**I. 수술 후 감염 예방**

1. **예방적 항생제 (Antibiotic prophylaxis)**

• Timing: 수술 1시간 전 또는 induction 시

• Duration: 일반적으로 24시간 (심장 수술은 48시간)

• Agent: 수술 부위와 환자 요인에 따라

2. **감염 위험 인자**

• Duration of surgery

• Blood loss

• Comorbidities

• Immunosuppression

3. **감염 조기 발견**

• Fever

• Wound drainage

• Wound pain

• Systemic symptoms

#### (2) 약물-저항성 감염 (Drug-resistant infections)**

**I. MRSA 감염**

1. **항생제 선택**

• Vancomycin: 1차 선택

• Linezolid: 대체제

• Daptomycin: 특정 상황

• Ceftaroline: 최신 세대

2. **투약량 및 기간**

• Vancomycin: 높은 용량 필요

• 치료 기간: 6-8주 이상

• Trough level 모니터링

**II. Multidrug-resistant Gram-negative infections**

1. **치료 옵션**

• Carbapenem (Meropenem, Imipenem)

• Fluoroquinolone

• Combination therapy

• Last-resort agents (Colistin, Tigecycline)

7) 예후 인자 및 장기 결과

#### (1) 양호한 예후를 예측하는 인자**

1. **치료 관련**

• Early diagnosis

• Prompt treatment initiation

• Appropriate antimicrobials

• Good compliance

2. **환자 관련**

• Young age

• Good nutritional status

• Intact immune system

• No major comorbidities

3. **감염 관련**

• Monomicrobial infection

• Susceptible organism

• No abscess formation

• Localized disease

#### (2) 불량한 예후 예측 인자**

1. **진단 지연**

• Late presentation

• Diagnostic delay

• Advanced disease at diagnosis

2. **환자 요인**

• Advanced age

• Severe comorbidities

• Immunocompromise

• Poor nutritional status

3. **감염 특성**

• Organism virulence

• Polymicrobial infection

• Antibiotic resistance

• Extensive disease

#### (3) 장기 추적 관찰**

**I. Follow-up strategy**

1. **임상적 평가**

• 월 1회 (초기 3개월)

• 이후 분기별 (6-12개월)

• 연 1회 (1년 이후)

2. **방사선학적 평가**

• 치료 6-8주 후 첫 X-ray

• 이후 3개월 간격

• MRI: 치료 반응 평가 시

3. **합병증 감시**

• Neurological status

• Spinal deformity

• Adjacent segment disease

• Chronic pain

8) 공중 보건 및 감염 관리

#### (1) 감염 통제 (Infection control)**

**I. 병원 환경**

1. **Standard precautions**

• Hand hygiene

• Personal protective equipment

• Proper disposal of materials

2. **Contact precautions**

• Drainage precautions if applicable

• Equipment handling

• Environmental cleaning

#### (2) 취약 집단의 관리

**I. 고위험 환자군**

1. **재원 기간 최소화**

• Early mobilization

• Rapid diagnosis

• Timely intervention

2. **추가 감염 예방**

• Prophylactic measures

• Screening for other infections

• Immunization (where appropriate)

#### (3) 의료 기관의 감시 체계 (Surveillance system)**

**I. Epidemiological monitoring**

1. **감염 추적**

• Type of organisms

• Antibiotic resistance patterns

• Outcome tracking

• Trend analysis

2. **Quality improvement**

• Diagnostic protocols

• Treatment guidelines

• Infection prevention measures

• Outcome assessment

Take-Home Message

본 단원(특이성 감염)의 핵심 내용을 숙지하고, 분류 체계, 치료 프로토콜, 합병증을 중점적으로 학습하시기 바랍니다.
